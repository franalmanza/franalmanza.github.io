I am fascinated by the things we leave behind, the things we disregard, and the things we ignore. I appreciate the Bauhaus manifesto and sensibilities, and so ensure that craft is incorporated as an element in my work by making use of printmaking techniques. I believe in the experimentation process, and rejoice in unexpected results and happy accidents. I am a dog lover and coffee drinker. I change my hair color more than I change my car's oil, and I always laugh at my own jokes.
