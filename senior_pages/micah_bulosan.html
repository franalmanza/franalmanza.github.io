As a graphic designer, I believe that graphic design comes in different forms. It is important to be speculative and aware in all aspects; from problem, through process, to solution. I enjoy creating design that moves and breathes, design that creates a conversation between me, the design, and the world. I believe in breaking the boundaries and thinking in a different direction into finding a solution. Graphic design has this way of being omnipresent and diverse, as it is present everywhere; on print material, web, digital, packaging, signage, branding, advertising. We have this freedom to tap into our minds in a way that the outside world may not understand, but through graphic design, we're able to create a language for our thoughts.
