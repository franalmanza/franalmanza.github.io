Storytelling is the core foundation for all my work. I've always been fascinated how a story can create worlds for audiences to emerge themselves in, and form an emotional connection to that narrative. I feel that there is an intimate bond formed between people and the narratives they see or read. The other element to stories that makes them so powerful is their ability to teach, fairy tales being one of the most notable ways of which to do this.

Fairy tales by definition are a fabrication used to guide an audience into understanding a particular message. I used this distinctly vague construction method to make my fairy tale how I see fit. Using the stylistic flairs, historical context, and modern influences, I want to shed light to concerns and observations to contemporary society in the hopes to educate people on the effects their actions take.
