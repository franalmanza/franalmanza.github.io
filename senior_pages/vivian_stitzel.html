My work consists of a mixture of digital and analog explorations and finding that harmonious balance between the two. Although familiar with designing for web and motion, the majority of work I produce exists best in the printed form, which is where my true passion lies. Design gives me the opportunity to really immerse myself in an unfamiliar subject and to develop a deeper understanding of it. This which, in turn, along with my own personal experience allows me to create a unique point of view and message to share with the community. My primary focus this past year has been exploring and fully understanding how people have read and interacted with printed and digital texts in the past and present, and speculating how we will come to see, read, use texts in the future.
