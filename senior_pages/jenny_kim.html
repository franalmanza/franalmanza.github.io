I am a designer with a wide interest. I am always up for learning, exploring, experimenting, and having fun. Every experience is important. Everything past, present, and future is related.

We only pay attention to the beginning and the end. Yet, the process that happens in between plays an important role in development and construction. It is about the mistakes and the madness. It is about embracing the unknown and unexpected. Take it as an experiment. Be observant but open-minded. Be persistent but yielding. And, be ready to gain a new perspective that allows you to truly be alive and living, because to live is not about where one begins and one stops but what is in-between.
