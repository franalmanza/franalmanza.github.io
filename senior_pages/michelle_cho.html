As an artist and designer, my goal is to become the best visual storyteller I can be. Throughout time, stories brought and connect people together to better understand the world. Through my work, I want people to be inspired and entertained by the story that is being painted in my design and art. My work explores the impact of strong storytelling and how it can give a new insight or perspective. I decided to pursue design because I was always interested in visual communication and how images can unite people from different backgrounds.
