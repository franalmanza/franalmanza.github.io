I'm an illustration student and L.A. transplant by way of the Inland Empire. I like to think of myself as uncomplicated and unfussy, so I try to keep my work simple too. My work exists largely in the weird spaces between illustration and graphic design, fine art and commercial art, two-dimensional and sculptural space. My favorite topics include contemporary and ancient mythology, politics, and experiences that are shared among nearly everyone but never addressed out loud. I try to keep my media utilization diverse, but I often gravitate toward graphic methodologies like printmaking and ink drawings. I like silly topics and niche interests, typically with a sense of levity and humor.
