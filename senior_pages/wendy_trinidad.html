I'm procrastinating writing an artist statement. It's like I'm afraid of myself. I need to relocate my mind to the place where I think it's funny when I say the things that scare the hell out of me. It's like that scene in Harry Potter: the things that terrify us are hilarious when we dislocate them from their positions of power in our minds, and out into the world where they can size up to something funny. Can that just be my artist statement?
