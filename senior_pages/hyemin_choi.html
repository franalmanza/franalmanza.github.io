Most art that is critically acclaimed seems to be dark and brooding, always aiming to shock the viewer usually in a negative way. Personally I have never enjoyed them much, and have always been more attracted to the brighter, warmer colors that exude positivity, hope, and even humor. For this reason, the main goal of my creations is to make people feel joyful; or just plain have fun. This is not to say that I don’t understand the value of thought-provoking art, I just think that the lighter side should not always be dismissed for being easy to understand. I am mostly influenced by art created mainly for children, such as children’s books, or animation that is able to communicate to a wider age range, such as Studio Ghibli and Pixar.
