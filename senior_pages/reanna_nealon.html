As a creative individual entering the ever-changing world of design, I am fascinated by digital technology and the opportunity it lends itself to designers and entrepreneurs. Growing up during a time of digital innovation has inspired me to develop my own voice as I continue to search for my place in this world. With an infinite amount of information and resources at my fingertips, I often find myself suspended in-between the hybrid world of print and digital design. I have learned to communicate my ideas, thoughts and feelings by asking questions and speculating about what these ideas can become. Graphic design allows me to explore and cultivate my own interests, while giving me the chance to share this perspective with the rest of the world.
