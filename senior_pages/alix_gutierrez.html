My work explores femininity, sexuality, and the grotesque by pushing the boundaries between what is both comfortably and uncomfortably attractive. My illustrations question the conventions of what it means to be a sexual woman of color in a world still bound by patriarchy and Eurocentric standards of beauty. The idea of female agency that drives my work is the control self-identifying women have over themselves and the freedom to do and appear as they choose. At its core my work is about identity and the celebration of femininity, both conventional and unconventional.
